About Bees

Bees are fascinating insects that play a crucial role in our ecosystem. They are known for their incredible pollination abilities and their production of honey. Bees have been around for millions of years and have evolved alongside flowering plants. Their importance to our environment cannot be overstated.

Interesting Bee Facts

  • A honey bee visits 50 to 100 flowers during a collection trip.
  • Bees have five eyes - two large compound eyes and three small simple eyes.
  • The average bee only lives for 4-6 weeks during the busy summer season.
  • Bees communicate with each other by dancing and by using pheromones.

Why Bees Matter

Bees are essential pollinators that help plants reproduce. They contribute to the pollination of approximately 75% of global crops, making them vital for food production and biodiversity.
